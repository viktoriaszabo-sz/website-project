Great goals and sweet points - HPK defeated KalPa

Runkosarjaa oli jäljellä tasan kolmannes kun HPK ja KalPa lähtivät iltaan. Sarjataulukossa on puolenvälin jälkeen todella tiukkaa ja selvää oli, että pisteet kelpaavat molemmille. Voitot olivat ennen iltaa KalPalle 2-1, eikä kumpikaan ollut vielä hävinnyt kotiotteluaan toiselle kuluvan kauden aikana.

The city of Hämeenlinna turns 384 years old on January 19, 2023

The birthday will be celebrated in the Raatihuone's ballroom from 14:00 to 17:00. At the event, the city's culture and sports awards will be handed out, as well as the hometown award given by the Hämeenlinna club. Coffee service for the first 400.

Sign up for studies at HAMK!

HAMK's open AMK is suitable for you who want to complete university of applied sciences studies - regardless of age or basic education. Anyone interested in studying can participate in the studies. With studies, you develop your professional skills, learn what it's like to study at a university of applied sciences, and update your skills.

Häme castle

Built in the 13th century, as a military fortress. Now it is considered to be one of the biggest symbols of the city. The medieval festival of Hämeenlinna is held in it's surroundings at the end of every August.

Sibelius' House

In 1955, a street in the center of Hämeenlinna was named after the master composer, Jean Sibelius, and his birthplace was opened to the public in 1965. Along with the city, the Hämeenlinna Sibelius Society cherishes the master composer's legacy in Hämeenlinna.

HAMK, Häme University of Applied Science

The university's center is located in Hämeenlinna. Its programmes are coordinated with industry and commerce. HAMK also offers research and development services, professional teacher education, further and continuing education and studies in the Open University of Applied Sciences.
